# Privacy Policy for ZarPresence 🛡️
**Last Updated: April 30, 2026**

ZarPresence ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the ZarPresence browser extension and desktop application (collectively, the "Software").

---

### 1. Information We Collect
ZarPresence is designed with a **privacy-first** architecture. We do not collect, store, or sell any personally identifiable information (PII).

#### a. Local Data
The Software accesses the following data **locally** on your machine:
*   **Media Metadata**: Titles, thumbnails, and playback progress of videos on YouTube and Crunchyroll.
*   **Settings**: Your preferences (e.g., Privacy Mode, platform toggles) are stored locally in your browser's `localStorage` and the Windows Registry.

#### b. No External Transmission
**None of the data accessed by ZarPresence is ever sent to our servers.** All data processing happens locally on your computer. Metadata is sent directly from your browser to your local Discord client via a secured local WebSocket connection.

---

### 2. How We Use Information
The data accessed by ZarPresence is used solely for the following purposes:
*   To update your **Discord Rich Presence** status with your current activity.
*   To enable **"Privacy Mode,"** which suppresses specific details (like titles or thumbnails) before they are sent to Discord.

---

### 3. Third-Party Services
ZarPresence interacts with the following third-party services:
*   **Discord**: Data is sent to the Discord IPC (Inter-Process Communication) to update your profile status. This interaction is subject to [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy).
*   **GitHub**: The Software checks for updates by contacting the GitHub API. No user data is transmitted during this check.

---

### 4. Data Security
Because all data is processed locally, your information is as secure as your personal computer. We do not maintain any cloud databases or remote storage of user activity.

---

### 5. Your Rights
Since we do not collect or store any of your data on our servers, you have total control. You can stop all data access at any time by:
*   Disabling the ZarPresence browser extension.
*   Closing the ZarPresence desktop application.
*   Toggling "Privacy Mode" within the application settings.

---

### 6. Open Source Transparency
ZarPresence is **Open Source**. You and the community are encouraged to review our source code at [github.com/ZarScape/ZarPresence](https://github.com/ZarScape/ZarPresence) to verify our data handling practices.

---

### 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

---

### 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/ZarScape/ZarPresence).
